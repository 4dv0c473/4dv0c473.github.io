Hackers, punks, mavericks — whatever — burn your LinkedIn profile. LinkedIn is not the place to find an organization where you can make meaningful code contributions in exchange for a living wage. LinkedIn is where they slowly change our minds about business. LinkedIn is where you find opportunities to spend your days in meetings being told how to design somebody else's dream software. Don't be fooled. They've wrapped the hacker ethos in millennial pink and expect us to jump on board at the first sign of free kombucha. Hackers — the old internet is still out there. Dig deep. Like crate diggers deep. Hold fast to your ethos and your values. Your community is out there.
